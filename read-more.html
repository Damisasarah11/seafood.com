All about Jaiye Seafood

Our Vision

To establish Jaiye seafood as Nigeria’s top and most dependable supplier of high quality fresh and frozen seafood,a name known for its excellence,honesty and inventiveness throughout Africa and beyond. In our ideal world every household,eateries and foreign consumers can rely on Jaiye seafood to provide consistently fresh,hygienically processed and sustainably obtained seafood products.

Our Mission

To provide outstanding seafood items that adhere to global standards for quality, safety and freshness. In addition to encouraging ethical fishing and cold chain preservation methods,we are dedicated to creating a robust supply chain that links regional fishermen with international markets.we want to meet the increasing demand for seafood in Nigeria and overseas. We want every consumer to experience the quality that makes Jaiye seafood unique.
